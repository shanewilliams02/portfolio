Introduction

There is such a wealth of information about web accessibility it can be difficult to know where to start or quickly find what you need.

I've curated this list in hopes that everyone from novices to accessibility experts can find something of value regardless of their role.

I'm grateful to the a11y community and for sharing their knowledge and I hope this list can be a small contribution toward moving the ball forward.
